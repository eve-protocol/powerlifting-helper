| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|

## Weekly Structure

| Day | Focus | Main Lifts |
|-----|-------|------------|
| Mon | Heavy Squat | Squat (Low Bar) / Bench Press (Paused) / 2 acc |
| Tue | DL Variation | Sumo Deadlift (Paused) / Incline DB Bench / 2 acc |
| Wed | Squat Variation | Tempo Squat / 4 acc |
| Thu | Heavy Bench | Bench Press (Barbell) / RDL / 2 acc |

| Week | Singles | Backoffs | Volume | Notes |

## Notes

- **Accessories:** Reduced volume throughout, capped at RPE 7 to preserve recovery
- **Week 3:** Peak intensity week - don't add weight to accessories
- **Week 4:** Full taper - everything is light, save energy for Day 5
- **Sleep:** Prioritize 7-8 hours, especially Week 3-4
- **Nutrition:** Don't cut weight, stay at 77-78kg, fuel the lifts
